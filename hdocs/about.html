About Project Vishious


We are Project Vishious of Yggdrasil, created and run by David Rodgers. This website will have notes on the world of Yggdrasil, upon which the realm of REDACTED hangs, inside which David's novels take place.



About David Rodgers


Born April 26th, 1992, David has long had worlds upon worlds within his head. Most recently he has began crafting a world tree to hold the worlds within which his creations live. His most recent creation, Drako Morgolen, lives in the realm of REDACTED within which lies the continent of Sladridor. David was born in the Sooner Medical Center in Oklahoma City, Oklahoma and has lived in Hutchinson, Kansas for approximately fifteen years now. He lives with his mother and younger brother in order to save money and help out family. David's hobbies include and revolve around video games, roleplaying games, anime, and movies. His favorite video games are of the genres of rpg, sandbox survival, and monster taming. He especially enjoys these genre in multiplayer games he can play with friends.
